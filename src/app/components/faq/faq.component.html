Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Just a little bit more about Cynergy

Will attendance be given?

TL:DR;

Mostly sessions will be conducted after college. However,if in any case the sessions are conducted during college hours, then attendance will be given.

Will there be any cost to attend any session?

Absolutely not. All the sessions are free and accessible by all the registered members of Cynergy.

How frequently sessions will be held?

Mostly it will be twice/thrice per week depending upon the number if programs being organised by Cynergy except for exam and assignment weeks.

How can we contact the committee members in case of queries?

Person of contact are open to all the queries regading our schedule and work process. Their details are provided in the home page. Also you can leave your queries in the online platforms made available to you.

Who should we contact to for help on starting a project?

Our Core Commitee has the final say in all project related matters. In case of any matter regarding projects you can approach our core commitee.

How will the leaderboard points be calculated(for competitive programming)?

The points for competitive programming will be calculated in the following way.
1.Attend a session - 5 points.
2.Solving questions in session - 5 points per question.
3.Solve questions after session before deadline - 3 points per question.
4.Solve atleast one question in monthly coding challenges - 6 points.
5.Solved all questions in monthly coding challenges - 5 points per question.
6.Missing one session - (-2) points.

How will contributions to open source projects be calculated? Will it affect the leaderboard?

The contribution to open source projects will be counted depending upon the successfull pull requests made by the contributer. Yes it will affect the leaderboard because the points earned through contributions will be added.

How will the details of the workshops/sessions be put up?

We will put up the posters including details of upcoming events and it will be notified to registered users through emails.

Will a tech fest/hackathon be held by the club?

Yes they will be hosted by the club once in every semester. Also monthly coding challenges will be there to earn bonus points. ;D

What all does the club offer? What is its intended audience?

The club will hold competitive programming sessions very frequently which will help you enhance your coding skills. In addition to this we will launch various open source projects contributing to which can earn you points. Also we will host Ted talks on Tech which will be delivered by expert professionals in their respective fields. Our target audience are all those people who loves Tech. :D

Can we give suggestions/feedback? and to whom?

We promote very healthy culture in our club. Everyone is free to suggest and leave a feedback to anyone in the club and it will be considered as long as it is worth it.
