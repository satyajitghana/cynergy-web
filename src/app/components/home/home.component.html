What is CYNERGY?

Cynergy is the Ô¨Årst of its kind coding club at RUAS. The purpose of Cynergy is embodied in its core open principles - Collaboration, Innovation and Competition. Cynergy is a platform to promote learning and development of programming skills. It empowers students with competitive programming skills through activities like Hackathon and Share sessions.

Collaborate

"Peers and environment are among the crucial components of growth. Thus, we encourage students to collaborate and group together to create the required habitat for programmers."

Innovation

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower. Cynergy encourages students to come up with innovative ideas, and provides support for bringing those ideas into fruition."

Compete

"No Competition, No Progress. Competing with peers sharpens ones skills, and provides a feeling of exhilaration like no other. So let loose and conquer the leaderboard!"

Open

"In accordance with GNU's philosophy, we aim to be open and free. Progress does not occur when one monopolizes knowledge and discoveries. Collaborating with a vast community not only improves one's understanding, it also protects one's privacy and enables transparency, both of which are our priorities."
